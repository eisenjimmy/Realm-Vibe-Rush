This is a **paid** CraftPix product (~$5.50). Frames under `game/` were sliced from the **public marketing gallery sheets** on `img.craftpix.net` for local integration.

For personal demos this is typically fine; for **shipping / commercial** builds you should **purchase the pack** from CraftPix, replace these files with the official download, and follow their [file license](https://craftpix.net/file-licenses/).
